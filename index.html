About Dream IT 2k26

Dream IT 2k26 is the premier annual inter-college technical festival hosted by the **Department of Information Technology**. We provide a dynamic, competitive, and collaborative platform for students from across India to challenge their technical acumen, showcase cutting-edge projects, and foster innovation.

Our 8 meticulously curated events span the full spectrum of modern computing and design. Join us to network with industry experts, compete for exciting prizes, and turn your technical dreams into tangible reality.
