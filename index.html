About Department

Department of Computer Science

The Computer Science Department at Islamiah College (Autonomous), Vaniyambadi, was established in 1988 under Madras University. Over the decades, it has grown into a center of excellence, offering a comprehensive range of programs including B.Sc. Computer Science, BCA and B.Sc. Data Science.

With state-of-the-art laboratories, a dedicated library, and a team of experienced faculty, the department is committed to empowering students with the skills needed for the digital world.

About Dream IT 2K26

"Dream IT" is a State Level Inter-Collegiate Technical Symposium organized by the Department of Computer Science. It provides a platform for students from various colleges to showcase their technical skills, creativity, and innovation. The event features a variety of competitions designed to challenge and inspire the next generation of tech enthusiasts.
